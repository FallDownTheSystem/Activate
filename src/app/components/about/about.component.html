Activate!

Activate is designed to bring likeminded people together by sharing activities. For your convenience we ask to use your geolocation to filter activities that are near you, and hide ones that are too far for your taste.

Some tips for using the app


The grey bar on the left means that the activity belongs to you!
The yellow bar on the right shows the currently selected activity.
